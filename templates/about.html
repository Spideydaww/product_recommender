About The Project

A product recommendation engine is essentially a software that records an users' actions on e-commerce websites and analyzes the data obtained to make product suggestions that might interest the user. This can enhance the customer experience and even boost sales of the e-commerce website that makes use of it. Community detection can be used to identify products that are most likely to be bought together, thereby facilitating a product recommendation engine. Here communities will be formed on the basis of the information obtained from user purchase patterns.
